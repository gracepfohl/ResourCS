About Us

Our Mission


Our mission is to provide young students with easily accessible resources needed to succeed as independent Computer Scientists. We have all experienced a lack of resources available to expose and introduce young people to different areas of technology, particularly Computer Science. Our website is the solution to the lack of available, simplified resources for students.
