Additional Computer Science Resources


It is important to always get involved and join organizations that are available to you. Here you will find many resources for your path to becoming a Computer Scientist. We have compiled classes that you should consider taking in high school to get you introduced to CS and the STEM path, clubs and societies that you are welcome to join and get involved to further your CS knowledge and to network with other future computer scientists like yourself, as well as a career pathway that is pre-planned for you that you can adjust to your needs.
